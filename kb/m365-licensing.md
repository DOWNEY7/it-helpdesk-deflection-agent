# M365 Licensing

## Overview
Microsoft 365 licences control which apps and services employees can access. This article explains licence tiers, how to check your licence, and how to request changes.

## Licence Tiers Available

| Licence | Included Apps | Who Gets It |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| **M365 F3** (Frontline) | Teams, Outlook Web, OneDrive 2GB | Frontline / shift workers |
| **M365 E3** | Full Office desktop, Teams, Exchange, OneDrive 1TB | Standard office employees |
| **M365 E5** | E3 + advanced security, Power BI Pro, Phone System | Senior staff, analysts, managers |

## Checking Your Current Licence

1. Go to **https://myaccount.microsoft.com**
2. Click **Subscriptions**
3. Your assigned licence(s) are listed with expiry dates

Or in Microsoft 365 admin (IT only):
- Admin centre → **Users** → select user → **Licences and Apps** tab

## Requesting a Licence Upgrade

1. Raise a request via **IT Service Desk** (https://servicedesk.company.internal)
2. Category: **Software → M365 Licence**
3. Include: your name, current licence, licence needed, and business justification
4. Manager approval required for E5 licences
5. Provisioning takes **1–2 business days**

## Common Licence-Related Issues

### "You don't have access to this feature"
Your licence tier does not include this feature. Check the table above and raise a licence upgrade request.

### Office desktop apps not installing
- E3/E5 only: go to **https://www.office.com** → Install Office → **Office 365 apps**
- F3 users: use Office Online at office.com (no desktop install included)

### Teams shows "Contact your administrator"
Teams may not be enabled on your licence. IT must enable the Teams service plan.

### OneDrive storage full
- E3: 1TB included — review and delete old files
- F3: 2GB — contact IT for a storage increase request (chargeable)

### "Your account has been removed from this organisation's M365 plan"
Account may have been disabled due to licence expiry or leaver process. Contact IT immediately with your employee ID.

## Licence Renewal

Licences are renewed annually by IT. Employees do not need to take action. If you receive an expiry warning, contact IT to confirm renewal status.

## When to Escalate
- Licence not assigned after 2 business days from approved request
- Guest/external user needs M365 access
- Shared mailbox or resource account needs a licence
